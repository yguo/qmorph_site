01

需求采集 — 多轮对话

SMB 业主通过 QMorph 官网的智能对话窗口，描述自己的业务类型、服务内容、营业时间等。系统通过多轮引导，完成信息采集。

👤 用户：SMB 业主
qmorph.ai/onboarding
Setup Your AI Receptionist
Tell us about your business — we'll handle the rest.
Q
Hi! 👋 I'm QMorph. Let's set up your AI receptionist. What type of business do you run?
S
I run a dental clinic — Bright Smile Dental, based in Austin, TX.
Q
Great! A dental clinic. What services do you offer? (e.g., general check-ups, cleanings, orthodontics, emergency visits…)
S
General check-ups, teeth cleaning, whitening, fillings, and emergency dental care.
Q
Got it. What are your business hours? And how long is a typical appointment slot?
S
Monday-Friday 9am to 6pm, Saturday 9am to 1pm. Usually 30 min per slot, 1 hour for new patients.
Q
Perfect. Would you like QMorph to handle: ✅ Appointment booking ✅ Rescheduling / Cancellation ✅ Business hour inquiries ✅ Insurance questions — or anything else?
S
All of those, plus emergency triage — ask if it's urgent and give them our emergency number if needed.
Q
Excellent! Last question — what tone should your AI receptionist have? Friendly & warm? Professional & concise?
S
Warm and friendly, like talking to a helpful neighbor. We want patients to feel comfortable.
